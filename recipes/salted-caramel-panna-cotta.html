Salted Caramel Panna Cotta

Description

Learn how to make this delicious salted caramel panna cotta. It's quick and easy, but a classy way to end the night!

Recipe Source: Beau MacMillan, The Food Network, Recipe Site

Ingredients

Milk Chocolate Ganache

  • 8 oz heavy cream
  • 8 oz chopped milk chocolate

Salty Caramel Sauce

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Fleur de sel

Panna Cotta

  • 3/4 cup plus 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbs powdered gelatin
  • 2 cups heavy cream

Garnishes (Optional)

  • Salted peanuts
  • Caramelized popcorn
  • Whipped Cream
  • Milk chocolate shavings

Steps

  1. Boil the cream in a saucepan over medium-high, then pour it over the milk chocolate and whisk until melted. Pour one ladle of the ganache into each glass used. Place glasses in the fridge to cool.
  2. Boil cream in a saucepan over medium-high. Put sugar with a pinch of fluer de sel in a separate saucepan and heat over medium-high until it turns a caramel colour (6-10 mins). Slowly add the ccream to the dark caramel sauce and whisk together. Allow to cool.
  3. Pour 3/4 cup milk in a bowl and sprinkle in powdered gelatin. Heat the remaining milk and heavy cream in a saucepan over medium-high until simmering. Pour the mixture over the gelatin mixture and whisk until dissolved. Pour in the salty caramel sauce while whisking. Distribute evenly into the glasses over the ganache and cool in fridge for 2-3 hours.
  4. Once chilled, garnish as desired.
